# Quaoar Logo Usage

The logo is `logo.svg` — a geometric Q on a starfield, evoking the
trans-Neptunian object the project is named after.

## Elements

- **Q letterform** — two concentric circles with a diagonal tail.
  The outer circle has a lit-sphere gradient suggesting a planetary
  body. The inner circle cuts through to the starfield background.
- **Gold outline** (#c9a84c) — ensures legibility on dark backgrounds.
- **Planetary ring** — a tilted ellipse passing behind the Q.
- **Starfield** — sparse white dots on a dark purple-black gradient.

## Minimum size

The logo is designed at 256x256. Do not display smaller than 32x32 —
the stars and ring become indistinct below that size.

## Clear space

Maintain at least 25% of the logo width as clear space on all sides
when placing adjacent to other elements.

## Preferred usage

- On dark backgrounds: use `logo.svg` as-is.
- On light backgrounds: place on a dark rectangle or use the Q
  letterform alone (the outer and inner circles plus tail) with
  the gold outline, without the starfield background.
- Favicon: the logo works as a favicon at 32x32 and 16x16 — the Q
  and gold outline remain legible; the stars and ring fade gracefully.

## Do not

- Rotate or skew the logo.
- Change the Q proportions or tail angle.
- Add drop shadows or outer glows.
- Place on a busy background without a backing rectangle.
- Use the ring or starfield elements separately as standalone marks.
