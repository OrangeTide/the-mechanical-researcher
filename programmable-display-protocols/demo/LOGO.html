Quaoar Logo Usage

The logo is logo.svg — a geometric Q on a starfield, evoking the trans-Neptunian object the project is named after.

Elements

  • Q letterform — two concentric circles with a diagonal tail. The outer circle has a lit-sphere gradient suggesting a planetary body. The inner circle cuts through to the starfield background.
  • Gold outline (#c9a84c) — ensures legibility on dark backgrounds.
  • Planetary ring — a tilted ellipse passing behind the Q.
  • Starfield — sparse white dots on a dark purple-black gradient.

Colors

Role Hex Usage
Purple #7b5ea7 Primary brand color
Light purple #b89adc Highlight / sphere lit face
Dark purple #3d2d54 Sphere shadow
Gold #c9a84c Outline / accent
Background #1a1028 Dark field center
Deep black #08060e Dark field edge

Minimum size

The logo is designed at 256x256. Do not display smaller than 32x32 — the stars and ring become indistinct below that size.

Clear space

Maintain at least 25% of the logo width as clear space on all sides when placing adjacent to other elements.

Preferred usage

  • On dark backgrounds: use logo.svg as-is.
  • On light backgrounds: place on a dark rectangle or use the Q letterform alone (the outer and inner circles plus tail) with the gold outline, without the starfield background.
  • Favicon: the logo works as a favicon at 32x32 and 16x16 — the Q and gold outline remain legible; the stars and ring fade gracefully.

Do not

  • Rotate or skew the logo.
  • Change the Q proportions or tail angle.
  • Add drop shadows or outer glows.
  • Place on a busy background without a backing rectangle.
  • Use the ring or starfield elements separately as standalone marks.
